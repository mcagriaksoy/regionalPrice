Regional Price Calculator

Take the guesswork out of pricing! Our regional price calculator helps app, game, and software developers determine recommended prices for each country, ensuring fair and competitive pricing across global markets.

How to use?

  • 1. Select your country and enter the price you want to compare.
  • 2. Choose the currency (EUR or USD).
  • 3. Click 'Calculate Regional Prices' to see the comparison table.
  • 4. Use 'Show More' to see more countries.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

💸 What is an app price and why does it matter?
The app price is the amount users are willing to pay for your app. It directly affects your revenue and how many users you can attract.
🌍 What is regional pricing?
Regional pricing means offering your app at different prices depending on the country or region. This helps you set fair prices based on local purchasing power.
📊 What does this tool do?
This tool takes your base app price and suggests recommended prices for other regions. It helps you build a balanced global pricing strategy.
🛠️ What data are the price suggestions based on?
The tool uses indicators such as Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) and regional economic factors to suggest fair prices. More data sources may be added in the future.
📱 Which platforms does this apply to?
This tool can be used for both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play) apps. Both platforms support regional pricing.
🔐 Are my inputs saved anywhere?
No. All price inputs are processed locally in your browser. Nothing is stored or sent to a server.
📈 Why are prices lower in some countries?
Prices vary based on local income levels and purchasing power. In lower-income countries, the recommended price is adjusted accordingly to remain accessible.
