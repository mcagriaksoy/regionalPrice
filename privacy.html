Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Regional Price Calculator processes, why we process it, and how visitors can manage privacy-related choices on the site.

1. Data We Process

We may process information submitted through contact forms, basic server logs needed to operate the site, and analytics data only if the visitor explicitly accepts non-essential cookies.

2. Analytics

Analytics is disabled by default. If you accept analytics cookies, we may collect high-level usage information such as pages viewed, approximate device/browser information, and interaction trends to understand site performance.

3. Contact Forms

If you contact us, we may process the information you provide to respond to your request, discuss pricing audits, or support product inquiries.

4. Legal Basis

Essential site functionality is processed based on legitimate interest in operating the website. Optional analytics is processed only after the visitor gives consent through the cookie banner.

5. Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, to respond to inquiries, or to satisfy legal obligations.

6. Your Choices

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies from the banner and reopen your settings at any time through the footer link labeled Cookie Settings.

7. Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact regionalprice@zohomail.eu.
