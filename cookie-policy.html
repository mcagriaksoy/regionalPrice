Cookie Policy

This page describes the categories of cookies and similar storage technologies used by Regional Price Calculator.

1. Strictly Necessary Storage

We store a consent preference so that your cookie choice can be remembered. This storage is used to operate the consent flow and does not enable advertising or analytics by itself.

2. Analytics Cookies

Analytics is optional and disabled by default. It is only enabled when a visitor actively accepts analytics in the cookie banner. If rejected, analytics is not loaded.

3. How to Change Your Choice

You can reopen your preferences at any time from the footer by clicking Cookie Settings. You can also clear site storage from your browser settings.

4. Third Parties

If analytics is accepted, analytics requests may be handled by Google Analytics. Please review Google's documentation and privacy information if you enable this optional feature.

5. Contact

Questions about cookies can be sent to regionalprice@zohomail.eu.
